About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer that loves to be challenged. I have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails and other frameworks and libraries.

I'm a psychology graduate and was going to become a mental health counselor but all that changed when I decided that I could impact peoples lives to a greater extent with web development. I worked as a adminstrative assistant, where a lot of the skills I learned were easily integrated into my web development life. The skills I have aquired such as patience and time-management integrate easily but the ability to work well with others is a skill that is worth 100 times more to me.
