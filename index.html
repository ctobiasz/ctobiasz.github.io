About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer that enjoys a challenge. I have experience with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Ruby on Rails and other frameworks and libraries.

I'm a psychology graduate with a passion for mental health and self-improvement. I was in the process of becoming a Mental Health Counselor(Graduate School) but all of that changed when I decided that I could impact peoples lives to a greater extent with web development. Before, I became a software engineer, I was an Administrative Assistant. This job was completely different but it taught me valuable skills that help me with my web development career. Integrating patience, time-management and communication was simple but so very beneficial. The prize skill is the ability to work well with others. A unified team is a productive team. Besides programming, I love to spend time with my family, play video games, play with my dog, and enjoy nature.
